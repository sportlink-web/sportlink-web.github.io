Account Deletion

You can delete your SportLink account directly from the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Log in to the SportLink app.
  2. Open the Profile tab.
  3. Tap the gear icon in the top bar.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Follow the confirmation steps shown in the app.

Data that is deleted

When you delete your account, SportLink deletes the personal data associated with your profile, including your email address and profile information such as your favorite sports, availabilities, city, and description.

Data that may remain visible to others

Messages you sent in activity-related conversations are kept so other users can preserve the context of those discussions.

Need help?

If you cannot access the app and need help with account deletion, contact the Service Provider at hello@sportlinkapp.ca.
